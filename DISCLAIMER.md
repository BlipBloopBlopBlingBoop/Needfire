## The AI can be wrong

Local AI models hallucinate — fluently and confidently. That is why every
Needfire answer cites its source documents: the citation is the product, the
paraphrase is a convenience. For anything that touches health or safety,
**read the source, not the chatbot** — and treat even the source as reference
material, not gospel.

## Electricity, batteries, and fire

Building the Bothy means DC wiring, lithium (LiFePO₄) batteries, solar
charging, and possibly mains power. Miswired DC arcs and starts fires. Abused
lithium cells vent and ignite. Mains voltage kills. **Fuse every source
line.** Any mains-side work belongs to a **licensed electrician**, done to
your local electrical code. The build documents are a reference design, not a
permit, a certification, or a promise that your build is safe.
